Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 6 mini cucumbers
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • chili powder
  • 2 tbsp green onion, finely sliced

Instructions

  1. Wash cucumbers and soak in salt water
  2. Combine ingredients into container
  3. Chop cucumbers
  4. Put cucumber slices into container
  5. Put lid on container and shake well
  6. Enjoy!

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3-6 large bell peppers
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Tenderize beef, add salt and baking soda
  2. Slice peppers
  3. Heat olive oil over medium heat in large skillet
  4. Add onion, stirring frequently (3-4min)
  5. Add garlic, cook (1 min)
  6. Add ground beef, chili powder, cumin, and oregano
  7. Increase heat to medium high
  8. Cook until meat is browned (4-5min)
  9. Add tomato sauce, bring to boil
  10. Reduce heat to medium low and cook (2-3mins)
  11. Add cooked rice and 3/4 cups cheese
  12. Stir until melted
  13. Fill peppers with meat, sprinkle cheese
  14. Place in oven at 400F for 10-15 mins
  15. Enjoy!

Spicy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta (5cups)
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 green onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp vodka (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper

Instructions

  1. Bring large pot to boil, cook pasta according to directions
  2. In seperate pan, heat oil and onions (3-4 mins)
  3. Add in minced garlic (1min)
  4. Add tomato paste and mix (5mins)
  5. Pour vodka and saute (3mins)
  6. Pour in heavy cream, parmesan, and red pepper
  7. Mix in 1/4 cup pasta water, then combine with pasta and butter
  8. Garnish with basil and cheese
  9. Enjoy!

Dumpling Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 5 green onions, thinly sliced green and white seperated
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup white miso paste
  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1lb frozen mini dumplings
  • 3 cups baby spinach
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive and sesame oil over medium heat
  2. Add carrots, onion whites, garlic, and ginger
  3. Cook until vegetables soften (2-3mins)
  4. In a small bowl, combine miso paste, 1/2 cup broth, mix until paste dissovles
  5. Add miso mixture and rest of broth to pot and bring to boil
  6. Add dumplings and bring to simmer
  7. Heat until dumplings are are heated through (3-5mins)
  8. Stir in spinach
  9. Add soy sauce and green onions
  10. Enjoy!

Banana Oat Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Chocolate Chips
  • 1 1/2 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients, except chocolate chips, to a blender and blend until fully combined
  2. Pour batter into muffin pan
  3. Top with chocolate chips
  4. Bake at 350F for 19-23 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
